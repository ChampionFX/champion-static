What is binary options trading?

Understand the simple idea behind binary options, how they work, and their advantages over other financial instruments. A binary option is a type of financial option with a fixed payout. The payout is determined at the start before you purchase the trade. If your prediction is correct, you receive the fixed (and stated) payout. If not, you will lose your initial stake and nothing more. It is called a 'binary' option because there can only be two outcomes: you either win or lose.

Whatever market you trade in, always remember that you're not purchasing the asset itself, but a contract (or 'option') that determines how the asset performs over a period of time. This limits your risk and makes it easy for anyone to start trading.
